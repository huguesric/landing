Discover the world of roller coasters

Learn the differrents types of roller coasters that you can ride in the world forever more thrills !!

Name of coasters by size

Megacoaster

Roller coasters with inversions <60 meters

Hypercoaster

Roller coasters at least 60 meters high

Gigacoaster

Roller coasters at least 90 meters high

Stratacoaster

Roller coasters at least 120 meters high

Differents types of coasters

Seated Coaster

Classics roller coasters where guests are placed in a seat

Floorless Coaster

Roller coasters based on seated coasters but without floor under feet

Wings Coaster

Roller coasters with seats on the sides of the rail

Inverted Coaster

Roller coasters where trains are positioned under the rail

Flying Coaster

Roller coasters where guests are placed in a "Superman position"

Stand-up Coaster

Roller coasters where guests are in standing-up position

Launched Coaster

Roller coasters with a propulsion at the start
